Shifty Rivers

All you ever need to fish Missoula, MT!

Based out of Missoula, Montana, Shifty Rivers aims to help anglers of all skill levels make the most out of their time on the water in Western Montana. As licensed fly fishing guides, we consider Missoula to be our home water. This website is full of guides favorite flies, updated blog posts, links to various other websites with valuable information, as well as tips and tricks.
