Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Crust
  • Sugar
  • Pie filling

Directions

  1. Lay pie crust in a medium sized pie pan
  2. Cut apples into small pieces
  3. Add apples and pie filling into crust
  4. Sprinkle sugar on top of apples and filling
  5. Cover filling and apples with layer of crust
  6. Cook in oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees
