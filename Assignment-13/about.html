About

We consider Missoula to be the Mecca of trout fishing in the United States. With 4
famous rivers and countless tributaries less than an hour from our international
airport, there are endless options for every angler. There are also 4 fully outfitted
fly shops, and great lodging options around town to keep you geared up and comfortable
on your trip.
