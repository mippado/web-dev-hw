How to Make a PB&J

Materials Required

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Instructions

Step 1.

Lay two pieces of bread out and apply peanut butter to both pieces.

Step 2.

Add jelly to one of the pieces of bread.

Step 3.

Put the pieces of bread on top of eachother (jelly side touching peanut butter) and cut in half. Enjoy!
