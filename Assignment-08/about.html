Why a PB&J?

PB&J's are the best snack / lunch for a college kid for many reasons:

  1. They are inexpensive.
  2. They are high in protien.
  3. They are simple and quick to make.

For me, I eat a lot of PB&J's because I burn a lot of calories while
I am working out and they help me keep on weight. They are also great
tomake at night and eat the next day.
